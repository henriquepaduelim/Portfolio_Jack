Project Alpha: Brand Identity & Guidelines

A comprehensive branding initiative to create a distinctive visual identity from concept to final guidelines.

Overview

This project involved developing a complete brand identity for a new technology startup. The core objective was to create a memorable and versatile visual language that effectively communicates the company's values and mission to its target audience. This included conceptualizing and designing a unique logo, defining a coherent color palette, selecting appropriate typography, and establishing clear brand usage guidelines.

My role encompassed the entire design process, from initial research and mood boarding to conceptualization, iteration, and final artwork creation. I collaborated closely with the client to understand their vision and translate it into a distinctive visual system that ensures consistency across all touchpoints, both digital and print.
