Project Delta: Editorial Layout for Magazine

Designing engaging and cohesive editorial layouts for print and digital publications.

Overview

This project involved the complete editorial design and layout for a lifestyle magazine, from concept development to final print-ready files. The primary objective was to create a visually cohesive and engaging reading experience, balancing strong photography with clear typography and intuitive information hierarchy. The design needed to reflect the magazine's brand identity and appeal to its target demographic.

My role included conceptualizing page layouts, selecting appropriate typefaces, designing custom graphics and infographics, and ensuring consistent visual style throughout the publication. I managed the placement of all editorial content, advertisements, and imagery, working closely with editors and photographers to achieve a polished and professional final product suitable for both print and digital distribution.
