Project Beta: Marketing Campaign Design

A series of engaging digital and print assets for a successful product launch.

Overview

This project involved the comprehensive design of marketing collateral for a new product launch. The objective was to create a cohesive and impactful visual presence across multiple channels, including digital advertisements, social media content, and print materials such as brochures and flyers. The design strategy aimed to capture audience attention and effectively communicate key product benefits.

My role covered concept development, visual execution, and ensuring brand consistency across all assets. I utilized a range of design software to produce high-quality graphics tailored for specific platforms, adhering to brand guidelines while exploring creative solutions to maximize engagement and drive interest in the new product.
