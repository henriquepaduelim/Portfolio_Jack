SwiftFox Creative: Agency Brand Identity

Overview

I created this brand identity and logo for a fictitious creative agency specializing in digital marketing. I wanted to create a modern and distinctive emblem that could stand on its own with a contemporary sans-serif font that screams digital. The fox emblem is sleek and speaks to the speed and efficiency of the agency.

The cool tones of the colour palette represent security, trust, and longevity, and all three primary colours provide excellent contrast for print and digital applications. I also highlighted the word fox within the brand name for a pop of colour and so that “swift creative” is reinforced on second glance.
